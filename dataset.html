An Indepth Look Into The Data Sets
That Form The Foundation Of This Project

Data is the heartbeat of every analytical project and this one is no different.

The software will require the following data to perform its functions:

Appliance data:

The software will require information about the appliances used by users, such as the make and model, wattage, and usage patterns. This information will be used to calculate the electricity consumption of users.

Electricity consumption data:

The software will require information about the electricity consumption of users, such as the amount of electricity used and the duration of usage. This information will be used to calculate the cost of electricity and the length of time that a user's purchases will last.

Solar capacity data:

The software will require information about the solar capacity of users' homes, including the size and orientation of the roof, shading patterns, and the availability of electrical connections. This information will be used to calculate the potential for solar energy generation.

Vendor data:

The software will require information about solar equipment vendors, including their location, product offerings, and contact information. This information will be used to connect users with the nearest solar equipment vendor.

The software will collect this data from users through user interfaces and APIs, and will store it in a secure database. The data will be used to perform the functions of the software, and will be protected against unauthorised access and misuse in accordance with the data privacy and security measures outlined in this technical document.
